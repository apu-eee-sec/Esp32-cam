Connection Information

How It Works

This viewer uses WebRTC to connect directly to your ESP32-CAM without requiring port forwarding:

  1. Your ESP32-CAM connects to a signaling server and registers with a unique ID
  2. Enter that ID in the field above and click "Connect"
  3. The viewer establishes a peer-to-peer connection with your camera
  4. Video streams directly from the camera to your browser

Setup Instructions

  1. Program your ESP32-CAM with the WebRTC firmware (see GitHub repository)
  2. Power on your ESP32-CAM and connect it to WiFi
  3. Note the camera ID displayed on the serial monitor
  4. Enter that ID in the field above and click "Connect"
